Welcome to SuperGeocoder

This is an online geocoding service that converts any address column in your file to latitude and longitude.

Once uploading your file, you'll be able to view your table right away on this website, or feel free to download the converted version of your file.

In addition, mapping service is also available for each address, empowering you to view each site's exact location and investigate more insights!

However, in order for the service to process your file, there has to be a coloumn named 'Address' or 'Add' (letter case doesn't matter). Click here to see an example of valid file.

Currently supported file formats: csv and xlsx


Click the 'Choose file' button below to pick your file, and then click 'Submit' to upload it.

Thank you for using this web service.
